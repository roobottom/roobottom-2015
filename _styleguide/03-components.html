Archive meta

The .archive-meta component is used to present a key to link totals and colours used in the archives section.

65 diary posts 57 galleries 104 notes

Gallery

The .gallery component is used when two or more images need to be contained together. It uses javascript to achieve a brick wall effect.

Pagination

The .pagination component is used at the foot of posts to link users to the previous and next items. It also gives an indication of how much time has elapsed since the posts were added, relative to the current one.

  • ⇐ Previous noteArundel Castle 10KA day previously
  • Next note ⇒She's growing up5 days later

Quicklinks

The .quicklinks component provides a minimalist set of stacked links designed to present a lot of url destinations with the minimum of fuss.

  • Burntwood

  • Wolverhampton

The .quicklinks component can also be used with the post-type colours and time element for lists of posts.

  • A Gallery Sun, Sep 13
  • A Note Thu, Sep 10
  • A Diary post Sat, Sep 5
